Page Elizabeth Lucky

Miss Page Lucky is a singer for hire based in the Big Apple in the heart of New York City. A classically trained singer she specializes in jazz, big band, and theater repertoire that’s fun and entertaining. Though she’s a NYC singer, she travels the states and internationally performing with piano, small and large bands. For booking inquiries, please visit the contact page.

Singer For Hire

From the US to Greece, Italy, Germany and the United Kingdom, Page has performed around the world. From private events for smaller groups to opera houses that hold thousands, she has 8 years of performance experience. Trained to perform with both a live band and full orchestra, she can provide your attendees with a unique experience. Cultivate a memorable show and pick from popular jazz songs, old school American favorites, opera arias, and French, German, and Italian repertoire for your event.
