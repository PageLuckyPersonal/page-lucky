True Love Song Cole Porter

Originally written for the film High Society, True Love has remained a great classic in America's song history. Bing Crosby with his crooning, low mellow voice was the first to bring True Love to stardom. Since then several famous singers have covered this famous American song. Since the MGM 1956 release of High Society, it's hard to keep up with the singers covering this beloved composition.

Part of the secret to this lasting favorite is the simple melody. Songs with simple melody and accompaniment, paired with touching lyrics seem to always find their place in history. The True Love song lyrics are sweet, simple and feature strophic repetition. These are the elements that gave some of our retro song favorites their true staying power. With the text "So I give to you, and you give to me", it comes as no surprise that it's a wedding song favorite.
