About Page Lucky

Page Lucky is an opera singer and wedding singer for hire currently residing in New York City. She sings a vast array of repertoire from musical theater, opera music, classic musical theater, and jazz repertoire. Page is a classical musician specialized in opera music as an opera singer and soprano, but frequently a performer for private events, and a singer for private parties. She's also a wedding singer, and sings for government events. A frequent jazz singer in Michigan with Arbor Opera Theater she has a vast knowledge of jazz repertoire, classical operetta, and classical musical theater favorites which always receive rave reviews from audiences.

Where Will a Classical Singer Travel?

Page will travel to any country, any state, and any city. Her most frequented locations are typically Honolulu, The Big Island, Napoli Italy, London, Greece, and small and large cities in the United States. Page frequently travels to far off venues and happens to love that aspect of her job. Ask and you will receive!

Planning the Music Theme or Repertoire

You do not have to plan the musical theme or repertoire for your event. The singer will select repertoire that's appropriate for the setting or mood that you want to create. Any request for a specific song or musical number you want will be considered. If you want a specific song performed at a party, wedding or special occasion, we're happy to provide it for you. The singer does have the final say on whether or not a song is possible, but our goal is to make you happy with the overall performance. Please rest assured that if for any reason we deny your request, it's only because we want your event to be spectacular. In the very rare occasion that we can not accept a song request, we will be sure to notify you ahead of time. We are happy to explore other songs that you may want prefer in exchange of anything we can not accept.

How and When Does Page Travel?

Page travels year around and can come during any season or holiday. In order for the singer to be rested and ready for showtime, we ask that you provide housing the night before and after an event. The company hiring Miss Lucky is responsible for any travel costs. This includes but is not limited to airfare, train tickets, buss fare, car rental if required, and a daily per dium for meals.

Will Page Attend Our Gatherings?

Page enjoys meeting new people and networking! If you would like to invite Miss Lucky to shared meals with your colleagues, friends, and business partners, you are welcome to do so. In order to care for her voice and provide the best entertainment for your event, please understand if she may need to leave at some point to be rested. Getting to know and mingle with your attendees is beneficial to the singer too.
