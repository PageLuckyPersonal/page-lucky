Page Lucky Returns to Sing at Esplanade

Where do opera standards, evening gowns, chic vintage high-ceilinged rooms, and excited audiences come together? On 74th street and West End Ave in New York at The Esplanade of course! Page Lucky returns again to the Esplanade to perform as a Strauss soprano with Die Fledermaus highlights, as Susanna in Le Nozze di Figaro, and singing classic theater song favorites too. With a nicely sized hall for performances, a beautiful black piano, and an eager audience of classical music attendees; Page Lucky returns a second time to sing classical and crossover singer favorites at this beautiful venue. The audience's favorites at the performance by local New York opera singer Page Lucky were, Mein Herr Marquis, Deh Vieni Non Tardar, Sull'aria and Moon River.

As a singer for hire in New York Page Lucky is always looking for new venues both locally and internationally. Please email with further questions or to hire a singer today.
