The Telephone Opera

Page Lucky will be playing Lucy with New York Lyric Theater Opera in their upcoming production of "The Medium". A great comic opera and a favorite amongst experienced and casual opera listeners because of it's run time, this will be a fun night of opera for all. As the telephone singer Page will play the character of Lucy which is a coloratura soprano role.

Run Dates: Feb. 16th and 17th

Tickets for sale $45 each and can be purchased at the door.

NOLA Studios: 244 W. 54th/8th/Broadway, 11th floor, Studio C
