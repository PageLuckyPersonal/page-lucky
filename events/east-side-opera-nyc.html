East Side Opera Performance Extravaganza

Opera singer and coloratura soprano Page Lucky performs on the East Side in New York for a closed performance of Verdi and Puccini favorites. Scenes from Rigoletto, Die Fledermaus, and Carmen were a few of the scenes performed for an eager audience of opera lovers of all ages. Of attendees favorites in particular from Miss Lucky were a love duet between Michaela and Jose from Bizet’s Carmen, and a Giuseppe Verdi aria from Rigoletto, Caro Nome.

Page also played a Chopin piano piece for the audience, a particular favorite amongst Chopin lovers entitled Prelude. If you didn’t receive an invitation to the private event, try visiting during the week after 6 PM where occasionally you can find Page practicing on the grand piano in the main entrance hall. Eager listeners in this venue don’t mind hearing new piano pieces and witnessing the practice and dedication required to master popular piano classics.
