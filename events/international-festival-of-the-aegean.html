International Festival of the Aegean in Syros Greece

Page will be performing this Summer in a variety of roles with The International Festival of the Aegean in Greece. Music to be performed includes the Queen of Night from Die Zauberflote, Olympia from The Tales of Hoffman, Sophie from Der Rosenkavalier, Gianetta from E'lisir D'amore, and as one of the slaves in Salome at The International Festival of the Aegean. Page will have a lengthy stay on the island of Syros in Greece where the performances are to be held in various venues and also in the local and historical opera house.
