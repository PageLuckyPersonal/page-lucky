Le Nozze di Figaro with Arbor Opera

Page will be playing the soubrette role of Barbarina with Arbor Opera in their upcoming Le Nozze di Figaro production this Summer in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Barbarina is a young teenage girl and servant living in the Count's home as a lady's maid to the Countess and sings the famous aria, "L'ho Perduta". She will also be costume designer for this production and those pictures are soon to follow in the sewing section of the blog. Tickets are for sale now! Come out to Arbor Opera's Mozart production to hear beautiful classical music and to see some artfully constructed historical costumes as well.

Shawn McDonald will be the stage director of this production and Warren Jones will be the musical director.

Show runs through June 13-16th 2013
