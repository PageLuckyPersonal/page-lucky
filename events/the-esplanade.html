Page Lucky Performs at The Esplanade

Page Lucky performs in Manhattan, New York at The Esplanade with pianist Inna Leytush, soprano Janette Lallier, and tenor HÃ©ctor Palacio. This was a closed performance to members of The Esplanade with Allegro Singers, an opera company owned and operated by pianist and opera music lover Inna Leytush. The opera concert consisted of standard favorites and featured a vast array of classical operatic music selections from for example Norma, Le Nozze di Figaro, Porgy and Bess, and Grenades.

Opera Arias performed by Page Lucky:

  • Sull'aria from Le Nozze di Figaro
  • Via resti servita from Le Nozze di Figaro
  • Mein Herr Marquis from Die Fledermaus
  • Champagne Song from Die Fledermaus
  • Summertime from Porgy and Bess
  • Quando men vo from La Boheme

Musical Theater Singer Selection:

  • It's a Grand Night For Singing

The opera event in New York was very playful and fun. The audience was quizzed on their opera knowledge. Instead of handing out a program, the audience was asked to name famous composers and operas, then according to popularity to list arias or songs that they knew well. Many times the audience guessed which aria would be performed, with Inna slowly guiding them to the right answer on their own. The Esplanade audience members were playful and a very reactive audience, happy to hear and to be part of the performance themselves.

Page Lucky is expected to return to The Esplanade again within the year in which more seating may be available, and the performance may or may not be open to the public.
