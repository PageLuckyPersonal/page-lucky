Page Lucky Sings Verdi Arias

Page Lucky will be participating with Arbor Opera's Viva Verdi Show in which singers will be performing famous scenes and arias by Guiseppe Verdi, a famous romantic composer, along with some other favorites by other famous composers. Page will be performing favorites as Gilda in Rigoletto, E'lisir D'Amore, Don Giovanni, Gulius Cesare, Der Rosenkavalier, and Die Zauberflote.

June 8th at 8pm at Westminster Presbyterian Church in Ann Arbor. >Concert is free and open to the public. Seating will be on a first come first serve basis.

Show on June 8, 2013
