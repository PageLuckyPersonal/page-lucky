Page as Susanna in Le Nozze di Figaro

Page will play Susanna in Arbor Opera Theater's upcoming production of Le Nozze di Figaro this May 2012 at West Presbyterian Church. The production will be accompanied by piano with a full cast and fully costumed. Shawn McDonald will be directer and Warren Puffer Jones will be music directing.

Free seating is offered on a first come first serve basis.

May 12, 2012 at 7pm

Westminster Presbyterian Church, 15oo Scio Church Road, Ann Arbor
