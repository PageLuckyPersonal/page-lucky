Christmas Concert Kentucky

Join us December 12th, 2018 for Page Lucky's Christmas concert at Eubank Baptist Church! The singer is returning to her hometown for a free concert for friends and loved ones. Page grew up singing in Eubank Baptist Church and this free concert is to celebrate coming home to longtime friends and family.

Eubank Kentucky Music

The concert is free for everyone and open to the public! Please join us for one hour of seasonal favorites with a live jazz band as Page performs standard favorites. The Eubank KY Christmas concert will start with classical music, followed by religious repertoire and feature many Christmas songs.
