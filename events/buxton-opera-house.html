Buxton Opera House The Sorcerer

Page heads to London to sing in The Sorcerer with Oxbridge Opera Company. The Sorcerer by Gilbert and Sullivan is a fun and comic opera or operetta with speaking lines and dancing. The production will be in the Buxton Opera House(seen in above photo) which is simply a beautiful and traditional space. You couldn't ask for a more beautiful theater.
