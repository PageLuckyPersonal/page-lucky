Don't Tell Mama Valentine's Day Show NYC 2019

Enjoy old American film music, romance, and drinks to accompany the Valentine's Day season! Join Page Lucky at Don't Tell Mama for a romantic evening of old school American love songs from some of the greatest American movie classics. Page's show focuses on love's greatest onscreen moments from our favorite Hollywood names. This NYC romantic experience will make you laugh, sigh, and reflect together. Watch love come full circle with starlets who paved the way with the greatest love songs from retro to modern films.

What The Valentine Show is About

Hear the love song associated with Cary Grant's love affair with beautiful Deborah Kerr. Watch Mitzi Gaynor fall in love with an older man, and learn what Amore is all about with Dean Martin in The Caddy. See love unfold in various times, places, and in different, colorful relationships. Many of these big screen moments shaped and enriched our lives from a young age. Elegant and thrilling, this inspired Valentine's Day NYC show celebrates Old Hollywood love with all the glitz and glam we adore from yesteryear.

About the Singer

Miss Page Lucky is a classically trained singer from Kentucky who has been living in New York City for more than 6 years. Though originally trained as an opera singer, she is a crossover artist who specializes in predominantly jazz repertoire. Page's jazz band travels both locally and abroad, featuring American jazz classics and big band music. From solo piano to full orchestra accompaniment, every show is a little different. For this romantic evening, she will be accompanied by a live jazz ensemble.
